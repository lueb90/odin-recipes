The best Veggie Lasagna in town

How to make a very nice veggie Lasagna

This is my best recipe for veggie Lasagna. It is based on mushrooms and miso and those take care of the "umami"

You'll need a little bit of time, but i promise, it is worth it!

Ingredients

  • 500g Portobello
  • 100g Tomato concentrate
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 onion
  • 2 garlic
  • 1 small piece of ginger
  • 1 can tomatoes
  • 1 tbls miso

Steps

  1. cut the vegetables very fine
  2. fry the mushrooms in a really heavy pan
  3. add the onion, the garlic, the ginger
  4. stir occasionally
  5. put the miso in the pan and stir
  6. add the tomatoes and let it sit for half an hour
